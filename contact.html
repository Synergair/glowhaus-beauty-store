Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the right products for my skin?

Take our Skin Quiz for personalized recommendations, or book a complimentary virtual consultation with our skincare experts.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 60-day happiness guarantee. If any product doesn't work for your skin, we'll refund or exchange it - no questions asked.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Free shipping on orders over $50. Express (1-2 days) and Next Day options available.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely. All GLOWHAUS products are cruelty-free and never tested on animals. Many products are also vegan-friendly.

Can I modify my subscription?

Yes! Skip, swap products, change frequency, or cancel anytime from your account dashboard. No commitments required.

Do you offer samples?

We include complimentary samples with every order! You can also purchase our Discovery Set to try multiple products.
