Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return an opened product?

Absolutely! We want you to try our products risk-free. You can return opened products within 60 days for a full refund. We limit this to 2 items per product per customer to ensure fairness.

What if a product caused a skin reaction?

We're so sorry if that happened. Please stop using the product and contact us immediately. We'll provide a full refund and our skincare experts can help recommend gentler alternatives suited to your skin.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

Never. We provide a prepaid return shipping label for all returns within the United States. For international orders, we offer partial shipping reimbursement up to $15.

How long does it take to get my refund?

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 1-2 business days. It may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank.

Can I cancel my Glow Club subscription anytime?

Yes! There are no contracts or commitments. You can skip, pause, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard or by contacting us. We believe in earning your loyalty, not locking you in.

Do international orders qualify for the guarantee?

Yes! Our 60-day happiness guarantee applies to all orders worldwide. International customers receive up to $15 shipping reimbursement for returns.
