How to Use

Morning Routine (Recommended):

  1. Cleanse face and pat dry
  2. Apply 4-5 drops to fingertips
  3. Gently press into face, neck, and decollete
  4. Wait 1-2 minutes to absorb
  5. Follow with moisturizer and SPF 30+

Pro Tip: For best results, store in a cool, dark place. A slight golden tint is normal and indicates active vitamin C.
