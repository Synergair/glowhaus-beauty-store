Shipping Questions

Answers to your most common questions

Free standard shipping is available on all orders over $50 within the United States. Subscribers receive free shipping on every order regardless of order total. Simply add $50 or more to your cart and free shipping will be automatically applied at checkout.
Yes, we ship to PO Boxes via USPS for standard shipping. However, Express and Next Day shipping options require a physical street address for delivery by UPS or FedEx. If you choose expedited shipping, please provide a street address.
We stand behind every order. If your package is lost, stolen, or arrives damaged, please contact our customer support team within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We'll either reship your order at no cost or provide a full refund - your choice.
If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us immediately and we'll update your address. Once an order has shipped, we cannot change the delivery address. You may need to contact the carrier directly to redirect the package (additional fees may apply).
All skincare products are shipped in beautiful, protective packaging with tissue paper and cushioning. For temperature-sensitive products, we use insulated packaging during extreme weather. Our packaging is also 100% recyclable and gift-ready.
Currently, we offer standard international shipping to Canada with delivery in 7-14 business days. Expedited shipping options for Canada are coming soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when express international shipping becomes available.
