Skincare Advice

How do I find the right products for my skin type?

We make it easy! Take our 2-minute Skin Quiz for personalized product recommendations based on your skin type, concerns, and goals. You can also book a free virtual consultation with our skincare experts for one-on-one advice.
💡

Pro tip: Not sure of your skin type? Most people are combination - oily in the T-zone, normal to dry elsewhere.

In what order should I apply my skincare products?

The golden rule: thinnest to thickest consistency. Here's our recommended order:
  • Morning: Cleanser → Toner → Serum → Eye Cream → Moisturizer → SPF
  • Evening: Cleanser (double cleanse if wearing makeup) → Toner → Treatment/Serum → Eye Cream → Moisturizer/Night Cream
💡

Wait 30-60 seconds between each step to let products absorb before layering.

How long until I see results?

Skincare is a journey! Here's what to expect:
  • Hydration: Immediate to 1 week
  • Brightening: 2-4 weeks
  • Fine lines & texture: 4-8 weeks
  • Hyperpigmentation: 8-12 weeks
Consistency is key! Use products daily for best results.

Can I use retinol with vitamin C?

Yes, but we recommend using them at different times. Use Vitamin C in the morning (it helps protect against UV damage) and retinol at night. This prevents potential irritation and lets each ingredient work optimally.

Ingredients & Clean Beauty

What makes GLOWHAUS products "clean"?

All GLOWHAUS products are free from 1,400+ harmful or questionable ingredients including parabens, sulfates, phthalates, synthetic fragrances, and more. Every formula is dermatologist-tested, cruelty-free, and made with sustainably-sourced ingredients. View our full ingredient philosophy.

Are your products vegan?

Many of our products are vegan! Look for the "Vegan" badge on product pages. All products are cruelty-free and never tested on animals. You can filter by "Vegan" in our shop to see all vegan options.

Are your products safe during pregnancy?

While our products are made with safe, clean ingredients, we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider about your skincare routine during pregnancy. Generally, avoid retinol products. Our team can recommend pregnancy-safe alternatives - just reach out!
